Francis Bacon：Of Studies

发表于 2016-02-18 | 更新于 2019-07-08 | 分类于 读书笔记 |

Studies serve for delight, for ornament, and for ability.

  • 翻译：读书足以怡情，足以傅彩，足以长才。
  • serve 在此处有服务，产生作用之意
    • Serve the devil 为虎作伥
    • Serve two masters 墙头草；脚踩两只船
    • Serve as a foil to sb 做…的陪衬

Their chief use for delight, is in privateness and retiring; for ornament, is in discourse; and for ability, is in the judgment, and disposition of business.

  • 翻译：其怡情也，最见于独处幽居之时；其傅彩也，最见于高谈阔论之中；其长才也，最见于处世判事之际。
  • 句型：Their chief use for …, is in … and …; for …, is in …..; and for …, is in …, and….

For expert men can execute, and perhaps judge of particulars, one by one; but the general counsels, and the plots and marshalling of affairs, come best, from those that are learned.

  • 翻译：练达之士虽能分别处理细事或一一判别枝节，然纵观统筹、全局策划，则舍好学深思者莫属。
  • 句型：For…, but…, come best, from those that…
    对于（普通人／事），…, 然而（更好的… 是来自于那些（更好的人／事）

They perfect nature, and are perfected by experience: for natural abilities are like natural plants, that need pruning, by study.

  • 翻译：读书补天然之不足，经验又补读书之不足，盖天生才干犹如自然花草，读书然后知如何修剪移接。
  • “完美”的多种表达：
    • flawless 无暇的 ex.the flawless skin
    • stainless 无污点的 ex.her stainless past
    • unblemished 无污点的 ex.unblemished reputation
    • impeccable 无可挑剔的 ex.a man of impeccable character

Read not to contradict and confute; nor to believe and take for granted; nor to find talk and discourse, but to weigh and consider.

  • 翻译： 活着不是为了去取悦他人，去荒废时间，去抱怨，而是平静地接受一切，还能不放弃地继续努力。
  • 句型：… not to …, nor to …, nor to …, but to …
