Bill Gates：Stanford University Commencement Speech

Veröffentlicht am 2016-02-18 | Edited on 2016-02-20 | in 英语精读

It turns out that a disproportionate number of those people are at Stanford.

  • It turns out that 结果是，证明是
  • disproportionate （数量或尺寸）不成比例的

That ran counter to our core belief.

  • run counter to 与…背道而驰

My visit to Soweto became an early lesson in how naive I was.

  • lesson in/on …的课

I was so taken aback by what I saw that I had to ask myself, did I still believe that innovation could solve the world’s toughest problems?

  • take aback 使吃惊，使吓一跳

Over the years, Melinda and I did learn more about the pressing needs of the poor.

  • the pressing needs 当务之需
    pressing 紧迫的，紧急的
